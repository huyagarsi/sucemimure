Olivia Deeble

Birth Name: Olivia Lillian Deeble

Place of Birth: Balwyn, Victoria, Australia

Date of Birth: 4 August, 2002

Ethnicity: Irish, English, other

Olivia Deeble is an Australian actress. She is the daughter of actress Kate Gorman and Matthew Deeble.

Olivia’s maternal grandfather is actor and comedian Reg Gorman. Reg was born in Sydney, New South Wales.

Olivia’s maternal grandmother is actress Judith Roberts.
