What does bundle listing mean on ebay

What does bundle listing mean?

A bundle listing is for related items you are bundling together into one listing…such as a cellphone, earbuds, and cord, or a camera body, telephoto lens, and filters. You are not selling a bundle in your listing.

How do I create a bundle listing on eBay?

Creating a Bundle Listing – The Steps:
  • Add the bundled item ASIN or link into the field “BUNDLE SOURCE ID”.
  • Press the blue button “ADD BUNDLE”. You can connect up to 10 different products in one bundle.

    What does custom bundle mean?

    Bundles are packages that contain a base product plus additional items or accessories. … Custom bundles are offerings that are created by a seller who is not the manufacturer of the main product(s) in the bundle.

    Why is it called eBay?

    The company officially changed the name of its service from AuctionWeb to eBay in September 1997, after Echo Bay Technology Group, Omidyar’s consulting firm. The domain name echobay.com was already taken by a gold mining company, so Omidyar shortened it to eBay.com.
