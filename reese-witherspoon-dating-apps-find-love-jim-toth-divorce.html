Reese Witherspoon Turns To Dating Apps To Find Love Again After Jim Toth Divorce

Highlights

  • Reese Witherspoon's friends encouraging her to get back into the dating game, perhaps through dating apps like Bumble.
  • Her close friendship with Bumble co-founder Whitney Wolfe Herd has helped Reese gain confidence both personally and professionally.
  • Although Reese dated a mystery man after her split from Jim Toth, she is taking dating seriously and being cautious this time around.

Reese Witherspoon may have shut down the Kevin Costner dating rumors, but she’s apparently open to finding another man following her divorce from Jim Toth. According to a new report, Reese’s friends are pushing her to get back into the dating game, and she may even be signing herself up for dating apps.

Reese Is Close Friends With Bumble’s Founder

“It’s been tough for Reese to pick up the pieces after splitting up with Jim but she’s crediting Whitney with helping her to refocus,” a source told Life & Style, referring to her close friendship with Bumble co-founder Whitney Wolfe Herd. “Get her mojo back and get back on the dating scene — on her terms and on her own timeline, of course.”

Bumble is a dating app that empowers women by allowing them to make the first move in initiating conversations; it was founded by Whitney Wolfe Herd in 2014 after she left Tinder, where she was a co-founder, aiming to create a more respectful and empowering dating experience for women.

“It’s a friendship that has filled Reese with the confidence not just to walk into boardrooms and make major deals but to open herself to new dating possibilities and options as a wealthy, powerful and suddenly single mom,” the source continued. “Reese could have given up on relationships entirely after the painful split from Jim but she hasn’t, even if she’s as picky as ever and still has sky-high standards.”

Reese Was Reportedly Dating A Mystery Man

Last month, it was reported that Reese was dating someone new, though his identity wasn’t released publicly. But now with reports that Reese’s friends are helping her get back on the dating scene, perhaps things have fizzled out.

“Reese doesn’t take dating lightly,” a source told Life & Style in May. “After splitting from Jim, she was determined to take her time and not jump into another relationship unless she was absolutely sure. She listened to her heart, and apparently, she’s now dating this guy exclusively.”

“There’s still a lot of secrecy surrounding her new suitor,” the source added about why the suitor’s identity was a mystery too Reese’s fans. “She’s totally into him and optimistic about where it might be headed.”

Reese’s first marriage was to actor Ryan Phillippe, whom she encountered at her 21st birthday celebration in 1997. The two quickly became one of Hollywood’s most beloved couples, tying the knot in June 1999. They had two children together: their daughter Ava, born in 1999, and their son Deacon, born in 2003.

However, their marriage encountered several obstacles, resulting in their separation in 2006 and their divorce being finalized in 2007. Various factors contributed to their split, including career pressures and rumors of infidelity.

The Legally Blonde alum Witherspoon started dating talent agent Jim Toth in 2010. They were married in March 2011 and welcomed their son, Tennessee James, in 2012. They shocked fans by announcing their separation in March 2023. While the exact reasons for their split remain undisclosed, insiders indicate that they simply grew apart over time. Reese continues to remain silent about what happened between her and Jim.
