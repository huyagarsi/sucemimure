Beulah-ECW Update, ECW Road Agents, &amp; More News


Beulah McGillicutty has not signed on with ECW just yet but WWE officials have talked about bringing her in, although she’s committed to being a mom. ECWWrestlingNews.com has been reporting that there is a good chance that she’ll make an appearance at ECW One Night Stand. Since Tommy Dreamer is in charge, if she ever wants to come in, she’ll be allowed to come in.

Former WCW wrestler Tim Horner was given a tryout this past week as a road agent. They are going to need a few new road agents for ECW and some people are getting tryouts.

As noted earlier today, the second ECW house show has been booked to take place on Sunday, June 25th in Elizabeth, PA at the Court Time Sports Center. The first ECW house show is set to take place the night before in Philadelphia at the old ECW Arena.
