Elsa Hosk Biography

Elsa Hosk is best known as Fashion/Runway Model who has an estimated Net Worth of $4 Million. Swedish fashion model, most recognized for her work with designers like Lilly Pulitzer and Dior. She was born on November 7,1988, Stockholm, Sweden. She appeared in the acclaimed Victoria's Secret fashion show in both 2011 and 2012, respectively. She was brought up in Sweden before travelling to the States to model. She became a Victoria's Secret Angel alongside Taylor Marie Hill and Martha Hunt. She was born to Pal and Marja Hosk and raised alongside two siblings. She began dating Tom Daly in 2015 and the two are currently expecting their first child together. Her zodiac sign is Scorpio.
