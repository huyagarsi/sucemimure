Bushpig Backpackers Hostel, Kampala Central Division, Uganda

Located in the heart of Kampala District, Bushpig Backpackers Hostel is a hotel with free wifi, airport shuttle, free parking and a 4.3 guest reviews rating.

Bushpig Backpackers Hostel has one of the most exquisite menus in the city, the on site restaurant treats guests to really fine dining. Guests can also unwind at the bar and enjoy an exotic drink.

Bushpig Backpackers Hostel has family friendly rooms that are well-spaced and are centrally air conditioned. High speed complimentary Wi-Fi access, flat screen TV, mini bar, tea & coffee making facilities.
