Lil Wayne Throws Insanely Lavish Sweet 16 for Daughter Reginae Carter

Stuntin’ like her daddy! Lil Wayne‘s daughter Reginae Carter got the most super of all Sweet 16 parties on Saturday, Nov. 29, when the Young Money CEO threw her an insanely lavish birthday bash in Atlanta, Ga.

PHOTOS: Most lavish kids’ birthday parties ever

Among those in attendance at the star-studded event were American Idol alum Fantasia Barrino, Real Housewives star (and former Xscape singer) Kandi Burruss, and Weezy’s protégé Nicki Minaj, who treated the crowd to a special performance of “Beez in the Trap.”

The theme of the party was “Winter White,” but Reginae had several colorful costume changes throughout the night. Arriving at Summerour Studio in a Cinderella-esque horse-drawn coach adorned with white lilies, she rocked a glittery, champagne-colored gown and matching cape.

Once inside the venue, she took the stage in a pink-and-purple sequined frock, which she accessorized with black high-top sneakers. She also tried out an electric blue dress with a ruffled skirt, and a white skirt-and-top combo paired with a personalized cape reading “Young Nae” in gold writing.

Wayne didn’t just give his girl a party, though — he also gave her a new ride. Two new rides, in fact. The hip-hop mogul gifted his eldest daughter with both a Ferrari and a BMW, each topped with a big red bow.

“Happy birthday to my angel, my princess, my pride and joy Reginae Carter!” he tweeted on Nov. 29. “16 years of being a reminder of how beautiful life can truly b.”
