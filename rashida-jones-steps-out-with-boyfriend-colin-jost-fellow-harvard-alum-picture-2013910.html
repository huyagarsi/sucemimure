Rashida Jones, Boyfriend Colin Jost Step Out for First Time: Picture

Looks like Rashida Jones is really fall-in' in love. The Parks and Recreation actress and her Saturday Night Live writer beau bundled up in black jackets and matching dark shades during a stroll through NYC's West Village on Monday, Oct. 7.

Jones, 37, kept her look on-trend in a leather motorcycle biker jacket (and matching purse), jeans and tan booties, while Jost, 31, kept his ensemble dark in a chambray shirt, padded jacket and dark jeans.

Us Weekly first reported on the pair back in September.

"She's known him for a while," a source told Us of the couple, who are both Harvard alum (Jones graduated in 1997; Jost was in the class of 2004). "Rashida is into him. She likes that he's funny."
