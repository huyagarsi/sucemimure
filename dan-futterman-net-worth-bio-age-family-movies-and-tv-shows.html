Dan Futterman Biography

Dan Futterman full name Daniel Paul Futterman is an American actor and screenwriter. In 2014, he was nominated for a second Academy Award for co-writing the script for Foxcatcher.

Futterman has also played Val Goldman in the film The Birdcage and Vincent Gray on the CBS television series Judging Amy.
