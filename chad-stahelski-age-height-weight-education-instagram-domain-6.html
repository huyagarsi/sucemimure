Chad Stahelski Age, Height, Weight, Education, Instagram, Twitter

Chad Stahelski is an American stuntman and film director. He is known for directing the 2014 film John Wick and directing its three sequels.

Stahelski also doubled for Brandon Lee after the fatal accident involving Lee on the set of The Crow and replaced Lee in the film. He has worked as a stunt coordinator and second unit director on several films.

He worked as a stunt double for his future John Wick star Keanu Reeves in The Matrix franchise, and later served as a stunt coordinator for the films.

Chad Stahelski Age

Chad Stahelski is 54 years old as of 2022. He was born on September 20, 1968, in Palmer, Massachusetts, United States.
