Willard High School Senior, Kallista Fisher, Dies in Tragic Crash

In a devastating turn of events, Kallista Fisher, a senior at Willard High School in Willard, Missouri, was tragically killed in a head-on collision with a school bus. The news of her untimely death has deeply shocked and saddened the local community.

Kallista was remembered as a kind, talented artist who had made a significant impact on those around her. Her sudden passing has left a void that will be hard to fill.

The multi-vehicle accident occurred near Elwood and also resulted in injuries to Harold Budnovich and school bus driver Katherine Lopez. Authorities continue to investigate the circumstances surrounding the incident.

The Willard community is mourning the loss of this bright young life. Kallista’s family has released a statement expressing their grief and gratitude for the outpouring of support they have received.

Details about Kallista Fisher’s memorial service will be shared soon. We extend our deepest condolences to her family, friends, and the entire Willard High School community during this difficult time.
