Rapper Bow Wow Has Revealed His Dream to Join WWE as Wrestler

- Bow Wow made the announcement on Facebook noting a career in wrestling has been his lifelong dream

- The rapper said this may come after releasing his last album then he would now focus on TV and film

- There were some WWE stars who welcomed him to the sport while some started early beefs seemingly getting ready for his arrival

Rapper and TV host Bow Wow is looking at a career in WWE wrestling which he says has been his "life long childhood dream".

The 31-year-old shared tweet on Monday, January 8, confirming that he will be dropping his last album before focusing on other stuff, which includes the WWE.

Voicing his desire to be a wrestler, he also saw that the news might appear a bit crazy but was ready for the next chapter.

He wrote:

"Now I know this might sound crazy... but after I drop my last album, I will focus on tv and film. And joining the @WWE. It's been a lifelong childhood dream to wrestle in the WWE."

In a follow-up tweet, the former 106 & Park TV host said he wanted to be in a tag team with the mask man Rey Mysterio and their tag team would be ready for any other duo.

His announcement led to a heated exchange with some WWE superstars ready to kick his a** in the ring while some were all about love as they welcomed him to the franchise.
