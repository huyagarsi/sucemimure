Longtime Nipsey Hussle Bodyguard J Roc Gets Emotional And Retires After His Death

The murder of rapper Nipsey Hussle took the music world by storm, and many were shocked at the incident.

It forced his longtime friend and bodyguard, J Roc, to write this emotional note on Instagram: “Never in a million years I thought I would be writing some s–t like this … we haven’t made a 100 Million yet … we was suppose to grow old and I call u big nose shoot jokes on you all day… but instead I’m here in tears writing this I wish I was there I would switch places with you any day the world need you here I’m so confused, lost , hurt I lost a brother, best friend, a mentor all I can here [sic] you saying now is If it was me, I would tell you, ‘N—a, live your life and grow.’ I’d tell you, ‘Finish what we started, reach them heights, you know?”

There was also a lot of speculation over what had happened, with some believing that Hussle was wrongly targeted.

The idea was brought forward when it was revealed that the rapper was not the only one who was shot in the attack, although he was the only one to receive fatal wounds.

Police have now apprehended Eric Holder as a suspect in the case.

The man has been formally charged with the murder, although further details have not been revealed to the public.

His motive remains unknown as of yet, and will probably not be revealed until the investigation has concluded.

It is known if authorities had to seek Holder out actively and he did not turn himself in as the police urged him.

The man allegedly had a heated argument with the rapper shortly before walking away and returning with a gun.

Holder is reportedly affiliated with local gangs, though additional details have not been provided by authorities so far.

A video of the incident has been released, showing the killer shooting Hussle several times before kicking his body and running away.

The reason for the argument between the two men remains unknown.

There has been some speculation over what they might have argued over, but so far nothing has been confirmed by authorities.
