Her Media Career

Layfield joined CBBC in 2014 and began co-hosting the design series, The Dengineers with Mark Wright. Later, she presented numerous shows such as The Dog Ate My Homework and The Playlist.

In 2017, she was named the host of The Playlist. Besides, she presented the Match of the Day spin-off show, Match of the Day Kickabout on CBBC.


Earlier, Layfield covered the weekend shows on Capital FM. In January 2019, she began to co-host the Capital Early Breakfast show on the network. Likewise, she is a reporter for The One Show on BBC One.

The multi-talented lady has also presented sports shows including BBC One’s Match of the Day and MOTDx on BBC Two. Additionally, she has worked as a host for the Premier League and Gfinity’s FIFA Elite Series.
