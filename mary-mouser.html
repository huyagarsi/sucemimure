Mary Mouser

Mouser in 2014, photo by kathclick/Bigstock.com

Birth Name: Mary Matilyn Mouser

Place of Birth: Pine Bluff, Arkansas, U.S.

Date of Birth: May 9, 1996

Ethnicity: English, Scots-Irish/Northern Irish, German, small amount of Welsh

Mary Mouser is an American actress. She is known for her role on the series Cobra Kai.

Mary’s paternal grandfather was William Austin Mouser (the son of John Garrett Mouser and Vivian Parker). William was born in Arkansas. John was the son of Austin Marion Mouser and Lucinda Hord. Vivian was the daughter of William Fremont Parker and Ida Cooper.

Mary’s paternal grandmother is Etoyle Kirby (the daughter of Claud Kirby and Ruby Edwards). Etoyle was born in Arkansas. Claud was the son of Jesse William Kirby and Emma T. Milam.

Mary’s maternal grandfather was Joe Allen Brumett (the son of Allen George Brumett and Minnie Mae Roberson). Joe was born in Lonoke County, Arkansas. Mary’s great-grandfather Allen was the son of John Clay Brumett and Audi Elizabeth Weaver. Minnie was the daughter of William Francis Roberson and Sarah Frances Orender.

Mary’s maternal grandmother was Mary Frances “Dolly” Cummings (the daughter of William Pernal/Purnell “Willie” Cummings and Amanda Bell Malcom). Mary’s grandmother Mary was born in Cabot, Lonoke County, Arkansas. William was the son of William Haute Cummings and Sarah Elizabeth Chance. Amanda was the daughter of Noel Nelson Malcom and Mary Frances Price.
