Kylie Jenner News - Us Weekly

Name:Kylie Jenner

Hometown:Calabasas, California

Birthday:August 10, 1997

About

Kylie Jenner grew up in the spotlight, becoming a household name in 2007 when her family’s reality show Keeping Up With the Kardashians premiered on E! She is the youngest daughter of Kris and Caitlyn Jenner, who also share daughter Kendall Jenner, and the half-sister of Kim, Kourtney, Khloé and Rob Kardashian.

In 2013, Kylie began her fashion career with Kendall by launching an exclusive clothing collection for PacSun named Kendall & Kylie. In 2015, the sisters expanded their fashion reach with Kendall + Kylie for Topshop.

Kylie shifted her focus to the beauty industry in 2016 with her own brand, Kylie Cosmetics, famous for its quick-selling Lip Kits, which consist of lip liners and matte-liquid lipsticks.

She was given her own short-lived spinoff show, Life of Kylie, in 2017. The series lasted for one season and explored Kylie’s business ventures, family life and friendship with Jordyn Woods. After the end of KUWTK, she joined her family on Hulu’s The Kardashians in 2022.

Kylie dated rapper Tyga from 2015 to 2017. She moved on with Travis Scott and welcomed daughter Stormi in February 2018. The pair expanded their family in February 2022 with the birth of their son. Though his name was initially announced as Wolf, Kylie changed her mind, revealing in January 2023 that his name is Aire Webster.

Us broke the news that same month that Kylie and Scott had ended their on-off relationship. Two months later, Kylie was linked to Timothée Chalamet.

Despite being named Forbes‘ youngest self-made billionaire in March 2019, Kylie’s title was revoked in May 2020. The publication alleged that Kylie had lied about her company’s figures and forged tax returns to “look even richer,” which she denied.
