Kim K. Explains Her Obsession With Nude Heels

#ShortGirlProblems — solved! Kim Kardashian loves herself a good nude heel, and now we know why.

In a post to her app entitled “Short-Girl Problems: My Top 3 Tips for Lengthening Your Look,” the Keeping Up With the Kardashians star, 35, gives her No. 1 tip: “Wear nude or clear heels.” Because, as she’s proven time and again, “wearing nude, skin-tone or clear shoes elongates your legs.”

Indeed, the mom of North, 3, and Saint, 9 months, stands at a petite 5-foot-3. But with barely there shoes — including the Lucite pumps in husband Kanye West‘s Yeezy lineup — she gets an instant leg-lengthening boost.

Rounding out the post, Mrs. West shared two more style hacks: Wear calf-length pants (“they make your legs look way longer!) and always wear heels — nude or otherwise.

“It’s not a secret that I wear heels 99 percent of the time,” the Kimoji creator shared. “If I do wear flats, it’s a momentous occasion, lol. I live in heels because they’re the best way to look taller.”
