KANDI BURRUSS CELEBRATES BABY BLAZE TUCKER TURNING 5 MONTHS

Blaze Tucker is celebrating five months of life. The youngest daughter of Real Housewives of Atlanta (RHOA) star Kandi Burruss was recently spotlighted while hanging with her brother, Ace Wells Tucker, and mother.

“Guess who’s 5-months-old today,” Kandi exclaimed online. The celebrity mom also shared pictures of her son and daughter with fans. “Your twin,” one fan exclaimed. “That smile, though,” another social media follower commented.

Kandi Burruss has found a lot of time to connect with her family because of current coronavirus social distancing rules that have postponed many events in the entertainment sphere. Still, some fans are convinced that the Night Angel on The Masked Singer is the Xscape singer.

“This is what I have to say about it,” Kandi said when addressing the notion of her being the Night Angel during a recent YouTube chat session. “Season 1, they thought I was on the show, they thought I was somebody. Season 2, they kept guessing my name. And now they’re doing the same thing for Season 3.”

Burruss went on to tell her fans, “I didn’t talk about it Season 1, Season 2, and I ain’t doing it for Season 3. You’re just going to have to watch their show.”

In all fairness, Kandi Burruss would not be allowed to admit that she is the Night Angel if she were indeed the masked singer. The purpose of the talent contest is, after all, for participants to remain anonymous until they are voted off or win the competition altogether.
