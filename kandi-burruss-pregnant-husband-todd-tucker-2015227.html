Kandi Burruss Is Pregnant, Expecting Child With Husband Todd Tucker

We know of several potential godmothers! Real Housewives of Atlanta star Kandi Burruss is pregnant, the singer-songwriter tells E! News. This will be the first child for Burruss and her husband, Todd Tucker, together, but she is already mom to daughter Riley and he is dad to daughter Kaela, both from previous relationships. 

“We’re so thrilled to announce the news of our bundle of joy,” Burruss, 39,  told E! “It’s a dream come true." 

The pair tied the knot in a televised ceremony in April 2014. 

“Our daughters, Kaela and Riley, couldn’t be happier about becoming big sisters,” Tucker added.
