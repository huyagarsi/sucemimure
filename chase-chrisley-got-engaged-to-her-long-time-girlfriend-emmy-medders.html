Chase Chrisley Got Engaged To Her Long-Time Girlfriend Emmy Medders

Chase Chrisley just proposed to Emmy Medders, his longtime girlfriend. On October 5, 2022, the former proposed to Medders at Tennessee’s First Horizon Baseball Stadium. Chrisley told People that it was the ideal night and that he wants to spend the rest of his life with Medders and that they can’t wait to start a family together. Chrisley proposed to Medders in a 12,000-seat stadium with 175,000 rose petals arranged in the form of a heart.

Medders expressed amazement since she had not been told of anything. The couple’s family had gathered at the spot to watch the event. Furthermore, she added that her father was suffering from ALS and that it was a great time for him to be able to come to Nashville and be on the field. Chase Chrisley proposed on one knee with a 3.5-carat oval diamond. The couple then celebrated at a neighboring restaurant, and Chrisley said that they would love to be married in Charleston. Todd, Chase’s father, has a few ideas, and when asked about them, Chase stated:

“My father has the finest taste of anybody I’ve ever met.” We’ve never had a designer or anything like that in any of our residences. He’s always done all of these things. So Emmy actually said, ‘Todd, just do anything you want because I know it’ll be greater than I could ever dream.'”

Emmy Medders rose to prominence when her connection with Chase Chrisley became public.

“I would appreciate it if you could remember my father in your prayers.” He began his first round of medicine, which is intended to delay the course of ALS.”
