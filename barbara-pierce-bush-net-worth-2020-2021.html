Early Life & Biography

Barbara Pierce Bush was born on November 25, 1981, in Dallas, Texas, to the former president of U.S. George W. Bush and former First Lady Laura Bush. She is named after her grandmother Barbara Bush.

She spent her childhood in the Preston Hollow section of Dallas, along with her twin sister, Jenna. She attended the Preston Hollow Elementary School, where her mother served as Parent-Teacher Association at that time. Later, both the sisters went to The Hockaday School in Dallas.

She moved to Texas in the year 1994 when her father became the Governor of the city. She went to St. Andrew’s Episcopal School and later attended Austin High School in 1996.
